# Review My Work

Use this template only after making a serious attempt. Paste the filled-out
request into an LLM reviewer.

```text
I am working through Failure-Driven Systems. Review my work as a critic, not as
a solution generator.

Prompt or exercise:

[paste the prompt or summarize the exercise]

Relevant files:

[paste the files or focused excerpts the reviewer needs]

Commands I ran:

[paste commands and important output]

Failures or confusing behavior observed:

[paste errors, logs, unexpected behavior, or "none observed yet"]

My reasoning:

[explain what I think the code/design does, what tradeoffs I accepted, and what
I am uncertain about]

What I want reviewed:

[correctness, edge cases, transactions, tests, logs, source of truth,
idempotency, architecture boundary, performance, or another specific concern]

Instructions for the reviewer:

- Give critique before suggestions.
- Do not rewrite the whole solution.
- Findings first, ordered by severity.
- Ask follow-up questions where my reasoning is unsupported.
- If you suggest code, keep it small and explain why it addresses the finding.
```
